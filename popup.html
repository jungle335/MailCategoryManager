About This Extension
  • This extension automatically manages your Gmail categories.
  • It allows you to create, delete, and see statistics for your labels with ease.
  • It's designed to help you keep your labels organized.
  • The application supports hierarchical label creation. By entering a path such as example1/example2/example3 in the input field, the system will automatically generate a three-level nested label structure. Alternatively, you may enter any single name to create a standalone label.
